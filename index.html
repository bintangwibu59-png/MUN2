Award Winning Tips

🏆 Best Delegate

  • Master the rules of procedure inside out
  • Lead negotiation blocs
  • Firm and solution-oriented speeches
  • Produce working papers/resolutions
  • Contribute in every session

🥈 Outstanding Delegate

  • Active in moderated caucus
  • High-quality arguments
  • Assist other delegations
  • Deep understanding of topics

🤝 Most Interactive

  • Frequently propose motions
  • Active note passing
  • Cross-bloc collaboration
  • Help draft resolutions

📜 Best Position Paper

  • Historical & legal analysis
  • Clear country position
  • Specific & realistic solutions
  • Follow proper format
  • Quote valid data
